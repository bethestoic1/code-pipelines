Best hosting for side projects and dev portfolios 2026

2026-03-02 · Code Pipelines

Where to host side projects and portfolios in 2026 without overpaying or over-engineering.

What developers need from hosting

Devs need: simple deploy (git push or CI → live), fast builds and cold starts, clear pricing, and optional custom domains and SSL. For side projects and portfolios, you don't need Kubernetes—you need a platform that stays out of the way (Vercel, Netlify, Railway, or traditional shared/VPS like Kinsta or Cloudways for full-stack apps).

Top options for 2026

Static/JAMstack: Vercel, Netlify—git push, previews, serverless functions. Full-stack / Node/Python: Railway, Render, Fly.io, or a managed VPS (e.g. DigitalOcean, Linode). WordPress / PHP: Kinsta, Cloudways—managed, performant, good for portfolios and client work. Pick by stack and how much you want to manage.

Picking by project type

Portfolio or docs: Static host (Vercel, Netlify) or a simple shared host. Side project with DB: Railway, Render, or a small VPS. Client or agency work: Managed WordPress (Kinsta, Cloudways) or app hosts with support. When you outgrow free tiers, compare monthly cost and upgrade paths before locking in.
